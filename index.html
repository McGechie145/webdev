About us:

Dundee city is one of the largest cities in scotland. Located in the heart of Angus and with a population approaching 150,000. We are known for our scientific advancements both past and present. See our history page for more information.

Dundee is home to two prestigous universities: The University of Dundeee and Abertay university. These are centers of learning for life/medial sciences and computer games respetively.

We are home to numerous cultural events, ranging from the vibrant music scene, literary, flower and food, or science festivals. See out visit page for more information.
